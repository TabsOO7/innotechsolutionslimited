Who We Are

About Innovo Tech Solution Limited

Innovo Tech Solution Limited is a technology company committed to building practical, reliable digital platforms for businesses across Africa. We are the creators behind GrabEats, Innovo Bus, and Innovo Markets – products designed to solve genuine everyday challenges in food delivery, transportation, and marketplace operations.

Our fundamental philosophy is that technology should simplify business operations, not complicate them. We collaborate closely with organizations to deeply understand their workflows, then engineer solutions that align with how they truly operate on the ground.

Product-Centric Approach

Expert Team
